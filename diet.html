Diet & Nutrition

At GYM HOOD, we believe that nutrition is just as important as training when it comes to achieving your fitness goals. Whether you're aiming to lose weight, build muscle, or simply maintain a healthy lifestyle, what you eat plays a crucial role in your results. That’s why we provide expert nutritional guidance to help you eat smarter and perform better—inside and outside the gym.



🥗 Vegetarian Full-Day Diet Plan

Goal: Balanced nutrition for energy, muscle recovery, and general fitness

  • 🕗 Breakfast (8:00 AM)
    • Oats with skim milk, chia seeds, banana slices, and a handful of almonds.
    • 1 glass of warm water with lemon (optional)

  • 🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
    • 1 bowl of fresh fruits (apple, papaya, or berries)
    • Green tea or black coffee (no sugar)

  • 🕛 Lunch (1:00 PM)
    • 1 cup brown rice or 2 multigrain rotis
    • Mixed vegetable sabzi (low oil)
    • Moong dal or rajma
    • Cucumber and carrot salad

  • 🕒 Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
    • Roasted chana or a protein bar
    • Coconut water or herbal tea

  • 🕖 Dinner (7:30 PM)
    • Quinoa or 2 chapatis
    • Paneer or tofu stir-fry with veggies
    • Small bowl of curd

  • 💤 Optional (Post-Dinner) (9:00 PM)
    • Warm turmeric milk or a handful of walnuts

🍗 Non-Vegetarian Full-Day Diet Plan

Goal: Lean muscle gain, fat loss, and sustained energy.

  • 🕗 Breakfast (8:00 AM)
    • 2 boiled eggs + 2 slices of whole wheat bread
    • 1 bowl of mixed fruits
    • Black coffee or green tea

  • 🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
    • Greek yogurt or cottage cheese (low-fat)
    • 1 banana or apple

  • 🕛 Lunch (1:00 PM)
    • Grilled chicken breast (150g)
    • 1 cup brown rice or 2 multigrain rotis
    • Steamed vegetables
    • Mixed salad with olive oil dressing

  • 🕒 Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
    • Boiled eggs (2) or whey protein shake
    • Handful of nuts (almonds or walnuts)

  • 🕖 Dinner (7:30 PM)
    • Baked or grilled fish/chicken (150g)
    • Sauteed greens or boiled veggies
    • Small bowl of dal or soup

  • 💤 Optional (Post-Dinner) (9:00 PM)
    • Casein protein shake or warm milk
